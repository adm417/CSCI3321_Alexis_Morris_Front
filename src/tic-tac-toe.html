Tic-Tac-Toe

Game Rules

  • Player 1 is X and player 2 is O. The two players take turns placing their marks in empty sqaures
  • The first player to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) of their own marks wins the game.
  • If all sqaures are filled and neither player has three in a row, it's a tie.
